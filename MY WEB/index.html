Why I Chose Software Engineering

From a young age, I've been fascinated by technology and how it shapes our world. The idea of creating something from scratch that can solve real-world problems and improve people's lives has always been incredibly appealing to me. Software engineering is the perfect field to combine my love for technology, creativity, and problem-solving. It's a discipline that offers endless learning opportunities and the chance to be at the forefront of innovation.
